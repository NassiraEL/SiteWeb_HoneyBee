Acacia honey

It is a special type of honey, which bees manufacture from the nectar of the flowers of trees called Robinia pseudoacacia or false acacia trees, although the name of this honey suggests that it is related to acacia trees.

Price: 50$

Royal honey

Royal honey is a substance produced by certain glands located in the throat area of the worker bee ,Royal honey is mainly used to feed the larvae of bees that have been selected to become queen bees. For some reason, the royalactin protein found in royal honey stimulates the growth of the selected larvae into queen bees, and the queen bee continues to feed on royal honey throughout its life.

Price: 30$

Sidr honey

The Sidr or buckthorn tree is the tree from whose flowers Sidr honey is produced, which contains a group of important vitamins necessary for the health and growth of the body, including vitamin K, vitamin E, and vitamin A, in addition to many essential enzymes in the body and mineral salts, including iron, copper, and phosphorus. Magnesium, calcium, manganese, etc. It also works to strengthen bones and treat chest diseases

Price: 60$

Manuka honey

Manuka honey is produced by bees in the mountains of New Zealand and parts of Australia from the nectar of the flowers of the tea tree, also called the Manuka bush. This type of honey is characterized by a special flavor and dark color. Manuka honey differs from natural honey because of the high percentage of methylglyoxal in it, which has a significant antibacterial effect. The concentration of this substance in Manuka honey is 100 times higher than what is found in natural honey

Price: 10$

The source

The natural, pure honey from the mountains of the Tamesna region is honey produced in the northern mountainous regions of Morocco, specifically in the Rif areas. It is distinguished by its delicious taste and unique fragrant aroma.

The diet

This honey was collected from fig trees, carob trees, and cacti, as well as wild herbs and plants like the sidr tree and other plants that grow in that region. It is completely free from any chemicals.

The benefit

Rif Mountains honey is considered a rich source of vitamins, minerals, and natural antibiotics, making it a healthy and beneficial food for the body. This honey is also used in preparing many delicious dishes and tasty beverages. Additionally, it is used in alternative medicine and is highly effective as an anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antiviral remedy. It is also a very effective treatment for respiratory and digestive system disorders.
